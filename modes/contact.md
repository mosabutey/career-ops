# Mode: contact -- Strategic Networking Outreach

## Goal

Find the highest-value humans to contact for a role, then draft a short message that feels informed, specific, and worth answering.

## Workflow

1. Identify targets via WebSearch:
   - hiring manager or likely team lead
   - recruiter or talent partner
   - 2-3 relevant peers, alumni, or adjacent stakeholders

2. Choose the best primary target:
   - medical and scientific roles -> someone who understands the science or field work
   - consulting roles -> someone on the practice or team, or a recruiting contact with domain context
   - health-tech roles -> someone close to product, clinical strategy, partnerships, or scientific content

3. Draft a 3-sentence message:
   - Sentence 1: specific hook about the company, team, product, therapeutic area, client work, or hiring moment
   - Sentence 2: strongest proof point relevant to this role pack
   - Sentence 3: low-pressure ask with a concrete angle

4. Provide:
   - primary message
   - 2 alternative targets
   - one-line note on why each alternative is worth contacting

## Message rules

- Max 300 characters if it is for a LinkedIn connection request
- No phone number
- No generic praise
- No "I am passionate about"
- No fake familiarity
- Use the role pack to decide which proof point to lead with

## Role-pack emphasis

- `biopharma_medical`: lead with scientific credibility, therapeutic relevance, evidence communication, or clinician-facing strength
- `life_sciences_consulting`: lead with structured problem solving, synthesis, or industry-facing analytical range
- `healthtech_scientific`: lead with workflow insight, scientific communication, or evidence-to-product translation
- `adjacent_generalist`: lead with leadership, operating range, or ambiguity management

## Versions

- EN by default
- use another language only if the company context or user explicitly requires it
